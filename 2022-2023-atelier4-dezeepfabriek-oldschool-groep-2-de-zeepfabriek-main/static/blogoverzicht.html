why do i suffer from flaky skin?

Have you ever suffered from flaky skin? This may just be caused by dry skin, but if the flaky skin does not go away, it may point to a persistent problem. If you suffer from flaky skin, you can first try applying a good body cream or oil. If this does not get rid of the flaky skin, it’s best to consult a dermatologist. A dermatologist will be able to tell you what to do next.

Catherine Moerman
